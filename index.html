About Clean Cleaners

Founded by Wilson Smoore, Clean Cleaners is a full-service cleaning company based in New York City. With over 10 years of experience, we offer comprehensive residential and commercial cleaning services tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our dedicated team uses eco-friendly products to ensure that your space is clean, fresh, and safe.

Our mission is to deliver top-notch cleaning solutions, exceed customer expectations, and maintain the highest standards of cleanliness and hygiene. Whether you need a one-time cleaning or regular cleaning services, we are here to help!

Our Cleaning Services

At Clean Cleaners, we offer a wide range of cleaning services for both residential and commercial clients. Here’s a breakdown of the services we provide:

  • Residential Cleaning: Complete cleaning services for homes, apartments, and condos, including dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and more.
  • Commercial Cleaning: Professional office cleaning, storefront cleaning, and cleaning for commercial spaces to keep your business clean and presentable.
  • Deep Cleaning: A more thorough cleaning option to sanitize and disinfect your space, perfect for seasonal cleaning or after renovations.
  • Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning: Expert cleaning for properties before or after a move to ensure that every corner is spotless.
  • Carpet and Upholstery Cleaning: Specialized cleaning for carpets, rugs, and upholstery, removing stains, dirt, and allergens.
  • Post-Construction Cleaning: Cleaning services for new construction or renovation sites, ensuring that your space is safe and livable after the dust settles.
